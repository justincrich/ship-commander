## Auth Module
Users can now log in with email and password credentials. The system validates input, manages secure sessions, and provides appropriate error feedback for invalid attempts.
Session tokens are stored using industry-standard encryption and automatically expire after 24 hours of inactivity.
Password complexity requirements enforce:
• Minimum 8 characters
• At least one uppercase letter
• At least one number
• At least one special character
